CEO & Founder

Abhinav Singh

CEO & Founder, Anath Shiksha Foundation

Abhinav Singh founded Anath Shiksha Foundation with a simple belief: no child should be denied education because of circumstances beyond their control. Under his leadership, the foundation has grown from a small local initiative to a pan-India movement impacting thousands of lives.

Anath Shiksha Scholarship Program

Rewarding Merit. Empowering Futures.

We believe every brilliant child deserves a chance — regardless of financial background. Our annual scholarship exam identifies the brightest students from underprivileged families and awards them full scholarships to pursue their dreams.

Quick Facts

  • Family income below ₹2,50,000/year
  • Government or low-fee private school
  • Orphaned children get 10 bonus marks
  • 80% attendance in last year
  • One application per student per year
